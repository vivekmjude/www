# imprint

dear reader,

this is my garage. door's up.

i tinker with things in here—code, music,
whatever i'm curious about that week.

the workbench holds whatever i'm trying to learn.

from here, paths lead elsewhere:
github for the code. soundcloud for the sound.
linkedin for the, well, linkedin.

no projects are "done". things go out and improve from there.

i'm glad you're here—come in. just mind the sawdust.

—vivek
